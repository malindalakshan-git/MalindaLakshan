Current Research

Buddhist Philosophy & Logic

I am currently engaged in an independent study of Buddhist philosophy, with a focus on its logical structures, epistemological frameworks, and the intersection of Eastern thought with modern cognitive science.

This research explores concepts such as causality (paticca-samuppada), phenomenology of mind (citta), and the analytical methods found in Abhidhamma literature. I am particularly interested in how these ancient frameworks of logic and reasoning can offer new perspectives on contemporary questions in philosophy of mind and artificial intelligence.
